# Support

Open TutorAI is backed by a growing community of developers, educators, and AI enthusiasts dedicated to building a better educational future through open-source tools.

Whether you're an end-user, a developer, or an educational institution, there are multiple channels available to ask questions, get support, and contribute to the project.

## Discussion Forums {#discussion-forums}

Engage with the community to learn best practices, explore technical guidance, or discuss the vision and roadmap of Open TutorAI. If you have a specific technical issue, please consider using GitHub Issues for targeted help.

### Discord Community

Join our official [Discord server](https://discord.gg/BTQtE2deEm) to chat in real-time with contributors and other users. It's the best place to stay up-to-date with announcements and participate in ongoing discussions.

- [#support](https://discord.gg/BTQtE2deEm): Get help with installation, configuration, or general usage.
- [#development](https://discord.gg/BTQtE2deEm): Ask questions related to contributing to the project or development practices.

Our [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Open-TutorAi/open-tutor-ai-CE/discussions) space is ideal for longer-form conversations, feature suggestions, project ideas, or general Q&A.
